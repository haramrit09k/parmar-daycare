Our Philosophy

Holistic Care, Not Just Supervision

Founded by Dr. Ankit Parmar, M.D., a child psychiatrist with over 10 years of experience, and Trupti Parmar, P.T., a licensed physical therapist — Bloom Holistic Academy nurtures the social, emotional, physical, and intellectual growth of every child, all at once. Our play-based curriculum intentionally weaves together nature exploration, social-emotional learning, and developmentally guided activities into every part of the day. We don't just prepare children for school — we help them fall in love with learning.

Programs & Age Groups

A Pathway for Every Stage of Development

6 Weeks – 11 Months  ·  $295/wk
Infant Care

Responsive, attachment-focused care in a calm, sensory-rich setting. Each infant receives close attention from a consistent caregiver. Our PT-trained team monitors gross and fine motor milestones from day one.

12 Months – 3 Years  ·  $275–$285/wk
Toddler Discovery

Language, motor coordination, and early social skills built through cooperative play, music, nature exploration, and purposeful routines. Social-emotional learning is woven into every activity throughout the day.

3 – 5 Years  ·  $265/wk
Pre-K Foundations

Project-based learning that builds literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking through play. Science experiments, storytelling, and nature-based discovery develop kindergarten-readiness and a genuine love of learning.

Why Bloom Holistic Academy

What Sets Us Apart

Expert Leadership

Founded by a child psychiatrist and a physical therapist — developmental expertise is built into every decision, from curriculum design to staff training to classroom setup.

100% Curbside Service

Innovative curbside drop-off and pick-up with dedicated outside staff and a smartphone app means your morning stays simple, even on the busiest days.

Nutrition Included

Our full-size kitchen prepares two dietician-approved meals and three snacks daily — mostly from scratch, focused on whole-food nutrition for growing minds and bodies.

Always Connected

Live-stream cameras via the Kids Report app plus daily photo updates and an open-door policy mean you're never out of touch with your child's day.

Our Commitment to Safety

Every educator at Bloom Holistic Academy holds current CPR and First Aid certification. Our newly built facility features secure entry, soft-flooring play areas, gated outdoor spaces, and documented daily health checks. Your child's safety is not a feature — it is our foundation. Fully licensed, insured, and subject to regular Texas state regulatory review.
