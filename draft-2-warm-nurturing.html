Our Philosophy

We're More Than a Daycare — We're Your Family's Partner

Parmar DayCare was built on a simple belief: every child deserves to be known, not just cared for. We take time to understand each child's personality, rhythms, and interests, and we share that with you — every single day. Our doors (and phones) are always open for a conversation, a question, or just a check-in.

Why Parmar DayCare

Why Families Choose Us

Feels Like Home

Warm, cozy spaces designed to feel like a second home — because for your child, they will be. We keep our environment inviting, safe, and full of personality.

Always in the Loop

Daily notes, photos, and our open-phone policy mean you're never out of touch. You'll always know how your child's day is going.

Growing Minds

Age-appropriate activities that build curiosity, confidence, and early learning skills — woven naturally into every part of the day.

Tight-Knit Community

Small groups, low ratios, and a team that knows your child by name — and by heart. We celebrate milestones together, big and small.

Your Child's Safety is Our Priority

Our space is licensed, secure, and staffed by trained, background-checked educators who hold current CPR certification. We conduct daily health checks, maintain clean and age-safe environments, and communicate any concerns to parents immediately. Your peace of mind isn't optional — it's built in.
