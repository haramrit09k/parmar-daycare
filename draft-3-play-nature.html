Our Philosophy

Holistic Learning, From the Ground Up

Bloom Holistic Academy is guided by the belief that children are born capable. Founded by Dr. Ankit Parmar, M.D. (child psychiatrist) and Trupti Parmar, P.T. (physical therapist), our educators act as guides — preparing rich, nature-connected environments and stepping back to let curiosity lead. We bring the outdoors in and take learning outside every single day.

Why Bloom Holistic Academy

What Makes Our Garden Grow

Whole-Child Development

We nurture cognitive, emotional, physical, and social growth — because children are whole people, not test scores. Every part of the day is a learning opportunity.

Expert-Designed Curriculum

Built by a child psychiatrist and physical therapist — not a franchisor. Every activity, every space, and every staff interaction is grounded in developmental science.

Outdoor Time Daily

Come rain or shine (safely!), children spend time outdoors every day — because nature is one of the best classrooms there is for curious young minds.

Meals + Connection

Two from-scratch meals and three snacks daily, live cameras via the Kids Report app, and 100% curbside drop-off — every practical detail is handled with care.

Safety & Trust

A Safe Space to Grow Wild

Safety and freedom are not opposites — they are partners. Our fully licensed Royse City facility features secure entry, soft-floor play areas, gated outdoor spaces, and documented daily health checks. Every educator is background-checked and CPR-certified, giving children the secure foundation they need to explore boldly.
